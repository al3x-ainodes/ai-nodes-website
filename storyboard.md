## ANIMATION NOTES
- Use ease-in-out for smooth transitions
- Keep text animations subtle (typewriter, fade, gentle slide)
- Workflow arrows should pulse to guide the eye
- Numbers should animate up (counting effect)
- Particles should be continuous but subtle
- Color palette: Cyan (#23DCF0), Purple (#9A23F0), Magenta (#F73674)

## SOUND DESIGN NOTES
- Ambient tech music throughout (upbeat but not distracting)
- Subtle sound effects for:
  - Button clicks
  - Notifications
  - Task completions (satisfying ding)
  - Workflow steps lighting up
- Silence briefly for impact moments
- Music builds during CTA section
