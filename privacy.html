1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and contact preferences when you create an account or contact us
  • Payment Information: Billing address and transaction confirmations. We do not store or log credit card details—all payment processing is handled securely through trusted third-party payment processors (such as Stripe or PayPal).
  • Business Information: Industry type, company size, automation needs, and goals you share during consultations
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, and correspondence you send us via forms, email, or other channels
  • Workflow Data: Information you choose to process through our automation workflows (e.g., customer contact details, form submissions)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and click patterns on our website
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics
  • Referral Data: How you found our website (search engines, referral links, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our automation services
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process payments, and send account-related notifications
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide technical support, and resolve issues
  • Customization: To personalize your experience and tailor our services to your needs
  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, marketing materials (with your consent), and service-related notifications
  • Workflow Processing: To execute automation workflows on your behalf using the data you provide
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our services and identify improvement opportunities
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our services (payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Business Integrations: With services you authorize us to connect with (CRM systems, email platforms, etc.) as part of your automation workflows
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AI Nodes, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Our retention practices include:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account deletion
  • Workflow Data: Processed in real-time; retained according to your workflow configuration (configurable by you)
  • Communication Records: Retained for 2 years for customer support purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 26 months
  • Legal Compliance: Retained longer if required by law or for legal defense purposes

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our services is encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • At Rest Encryption: Sensitive data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limiting who can access your data (role-based access)
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on reputable cloud providers with SOC 2 compliant data centers
  • Employee Training: All team members receive privacy and security training

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (authentication, security)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (only with consent)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. We honor "Do Not Track" signals from browsers.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information. You can:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restriction: Request limited processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us at al3xvip@protonmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Note: California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. EU/UK residents have rights under GDPR. Contact us for region-specific requests.

8. HIPAA Compliance

For clients in the healthcare industry who process Protected Health Information (PHI), we offer HIPAA-compliant automation solutions. This includes:

  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA): We sign BAAs with healthcare clients who need PHI processing
  • Technical Safeguards: Enhanced encryption, access controls, and audit logging for PHI
  • Physical Safeguards: Secure data centers with physical access controls
  • Administrative Safeguards: HIPAA-trained staff and documented policies

If you need HIPAA-compliant services, please contact us to discuss your requirements and execute a BAA before sharing any PHI.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our services may contain links to third-party websites, services, or applications. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to:

  • Review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access
  • Understand how third parties collect and use your data
  • Make informed decisions about interacting with third-party services

When you connect third-party services to your workflows, you authorize us to share data with those services according to your configuration.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at al3xvip@protonmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you via email for significant changes
  • We will post a notice on our website for 30 days before changes take effect
  • Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: al3xvip@protonmail.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message.
